Framework
Institutional Grid Cycle

The weekly structure that governs institutional price delivery. Every session has a role.

Monday
Opening Range
The week's initial price range is established. Gap behaviour and early bias signals emerge here.
Tuesday
Initial Balance
The signal on the day appears. Higher Highs, Lower Lows or false breaks confirm the weekly directional read.
Wednesday
D3 Midpoint + Reset
Price coils. The ignition window opens. Breakout-pullback-continue setups activate for trend trades.
Thursday
Extension · Reversal · Liquidation
The week's directional move reaches completion or reverses. High-probability momentum or exhaustion.
Friday
Closing Range
Institutional positions close. The week's range is defined. Next week's opening bias is set.
